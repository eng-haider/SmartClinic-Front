Last updated: March 2026

SmartClinic ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy and security of our users. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use the SmartClinic business management platform.

To provide our business management services, we may collect:

  • Account credentials (email address, encrypted password)

Collected information is used solely to:

3. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and at rest to protect all user and client data.

We do not sell, rent, or share personal information with third parties except as required by law or with explicit user consent.

Data is retained only as long as necessary to provide the service or as required by applicable law. Users may request deletion of their data at any time.

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. Contact us at support@smartclinic.software to exercise these rights.

SmartClinic is a B2B business management platform intended for business owners and their staff. It is not directed to individuals under the age of 18.
